Crispy tofu

with mushed potatoes

Description

This recipe is awesome for hood nights dinner. These tofu picies are soft inside and crunchy on the outside. The mashed potatoes with a bit of cream add awesomly to the spicines of the tofu.

If you don't like spicy food, feel free to do any marinade you prefer! Or just skip the chilli, play with the reciepe as much as you'd like

Ingridients

  • Tofu (300 g)
  • Potatoes (300 g)
  • Cornflakes
  • Cornstarch
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish spices
  • Spring onion
  • Soy cream

Steps

  1. Marinade your tofu with fish spices and soy sauce day before cooking, leave it in the fridge overnight
  2. Add your peeled potatoes to the boiling salted water
  3. Mix cornstarch with a bit of water and add your tofu picies to the mixture, choose an amount of cornstarch that will nicely cover your picies
  4. Take you tofu and put it in the mashed cornflakes, roll them around for complete cornflake cover
  5. Drop the picies into heated pan with oil and fry until golden
  6. Mash down your cooked potatoes with fork and add salt and bit of cream into it, finish by stiring in the cut spring onion
